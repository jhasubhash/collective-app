// TEAM COLLABORATION

Create shared spaces for your team. Organize documents in folders, write in markdown, and let the AI answer questions about your content. Ask "When was the farewell party?" and get instant answers with source citations.

Connect to local LLMs like Ollama or LM Studio for full privacy. Or use cloud providers like OpenAI, Claude, or Gemini. Your choice, your data, your rules.

No central server. Connect directly with teammates on your local network. See who's online, share your Peer ID, and sync documents in real-time. Your data never leaves your network.

Store scripts, configs, and technical docs. Ask the AI to explain your backup scripts or find that CLI command you forgot. Get syntax-highlighted code blocks in responses with full context.
